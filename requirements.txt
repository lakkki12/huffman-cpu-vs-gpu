# Project Prerequisites & System Requirements

## Operating System
* Windows 10 or Windows 11 (64-bit)

## IDE & Build Tools
* Visual Studio 2022 (Community, Professional, or Enterprise)
* "Desktop development with C++" workload installed in Visual Studio Installer
* MSBuild (v143 toolset or newer)

## CUDA Toolkit
* NVIDIA CUDA Toolkit v12.0 or newer (v12.6 / v12.9 recommended)
* Visual Studio CUDA integration enabled (installed automatically by the CUDA Toolkit installer)

## Hardware Target
* GPU: NVIDIA Maxwell, Pascal, Turing, Ampere, Ada Lovelace, or Hopper architecture GPU (GTX 1050 Ti or newer, with at least 4 GB VRAM)
* CPU: Intel or AMD x86_64 Processor (Intel Core i5-8500H or equivalent)
* Pinned Memory: At least 8 GB System RAM recommended
